Introducing

Pollock

A modern, simple and classy way to make polls

What is Pollock ?

With Pollock, you can easily create and acces polls. Everything is simple and user-friendly! Just create an account and log-in, and you'll be able to set up and manage your own polls. Once done, you can invite all your friends to answer your polls and view some really interesting stats about them. Pollock, a modern, simple and classy way to make polls!

Advance features

With Pollock you can not only create and access polls but also watch some really interesting real-time statistics about them. You can for exemple see the number of created and active polls in text and graphic formats. You want to know the number of user, which answered questions of your own poll? No problem, just access the poll's statistics page and let Pollock shows you all you ever wanted!

Responsive design

You don't have a computer or want to access Pollock with your Smartphone? You won't be disapointed in this domain! It is designed to provide an easy-to-use and modern interface both on computer and on mobile. Everything is automatic so don't pay attention you won't even notice that you're on a mobile when navigating through Pollock!
